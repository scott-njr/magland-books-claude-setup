Direction Two

Family Letter

Relationship-first IA

Inverts ecom hierarchy — earn trust before selling. The mission opens the page; books appear after.

  • "Dear reader" hero opens like a letter
  • Drop-cap body, signature, no cart in hero
  • Books shown as "here's what we've made"
  • Alternating editorial book rows
  • Newsletter signs off as a letter close

Direction Three

Catalog / Issue

Editorial-feature IA

Each book gets a full feature spread with watercolor backdrop. Homepage reads like a publication.

  • Issue masthead — Vol. I, No. 2, Spring 2026
  • Floating ToC dot-nav anchored on left
  • Per-book color wash echoes the cover
  • Pull-quote interlude between features
  • "Get the next issue" subscribe close
